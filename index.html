Breathe. You're not broken, you're just tired.

Burnout doesn't mean you've failed. It's a sign you've been strong for too long. This space was created to help you slow down, understand what's happening, and find your way back to balance.

Learn about Burnout How to Deal with My Burnout

Learn more about burnout in simple, real ways.

It's more common than you think.

Around 3 in 5 students and professionals report feeling emotionally drained or detached from their work at least once a year.

Recovery is possible.

With rest, reflection, and support, most people begin to regain motivation and energy within weeks or months, not years.

You don't have to go through it alone.

Talking to friends, mentors, or mental health professionals can make a major difference. Healing starts when you stop hiding your exhaustion.

This website is for awareness and support only. It is not a substitute for professional care. If you feel overwhelmed or unsafe, please reach out to a licensed mental health professional.
